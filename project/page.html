Messofy Aloo Matar is one of the most comforting and beloved Punjabi curries around. I learned how to make this spicy tomato-based potato pea curry from my Mother-in-Law, and it is now a favorite recipe in our house. Serve with some puri, roti or paratha for a meal that feels like a hug!

Aloo (meaning “potatoes”) and matar (also spelled mattar or mutter, meaning “peas”) join forces in this delightfully saucy curry. When I’m in need of comfort food, this is the recipe I turn to.
